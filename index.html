About Angie

Angie lives in Austin, TX where she enjoys the outdoor activities Central Texas has to offer, the many opportunities to meet and learn from successful entrepreneurs, the thriving tech scene, and of course the tacos - definitely the tacos.

Out of college Angie became a series 7 licensed stockbroker. In this role she learned the value of setting goals, being persistent, and thriving in a competitive, performance based environment. As one of very few women in the office, she quickly adapted and excelled in this environment.

After her first daughter was born, Angie made the courageous decision to homeschool her children. For the next decade and a half, she chose curricula, taught daily lessons and achieved measurable results. Angie considers the outcome her greatest achievement.

Throughout the time she homeschooled her children, Angie worked on various entrepreneurial and sales endeavors, honing her consultative sales techniques. It was during one of these projects that Angie became interested in digital advertising and began managing successful Adwords campaigns. She is a Google Certified Adwords Specialist.

Once she discovered the power of the internet to engage people, Angie knew she wanted to learn to code and create meaningful user experiences. She enrolled in Thinkful's Web Development Bootcamp and learned full-stack web development. Throughout this time, Angie met with a professional developer/mentor three times a week and worked on a series of web applications. Angie's language of choice is JavaScript. While she has the skills to build full-stack JavaScript applications using Node/Express, Mongoose/MongoDB, and Mocha/Chai, Angie particularly enjoys building web apps with React/Redux and utilizing the capabilities of version control.

Angie brings a unique skill set and experience to a development team. Her excellent interpersonal skills allow her to effectively communicate with a diverse group of clients and colleagues. Angie's passion for building successful businesses is refreshing, and her positive energy is contagious.

Allergen Free Recipes

Allergen Free Recipes is an app that allows users to search for recipes that are free of common allergens. Users may also add their own allergen free recipes. It was built using Node.js/Express, Mongoose/MongoDB, Mocha/Chai, Javascript/jQuery, HTML5 and CSS3.

Demo Allergen Free Recipe App

Find Your Next Journey

Find Your Next Journey is a book search app that allows users to find books or authors similar to others they enjoy. It includes a synopsis of each recommended book and sample searches for popular authors. It was built using AJAX, TasteKid API, Javascript/jQuery, HTML5 and CSS3.

Demo Find Your Next Journey App

My Chore List

My Chore List is an app that allows parents to create a list of chores including the expected time to complete each task. Children may check off the chores as they are completed. A count keeps track of how many chores are left to complete that day. It was built using React.js/Redux.js, Javascript,
HTML5 and CSS3.

Demo My Chore List App

Study App

This study app allows users to learn terms and definitions using flashcards. The terms are on the front of the card and the definitions are on the back. A user can hover over each card to flip it and reveal the definition. It was built using Node.js/Express, Mongoose/MongoDB, React.js/Redux.js, Javascript, HTML5 and CSS3.
